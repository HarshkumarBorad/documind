# Aurora Labs — Leave Policy

Effective from 1 January 2025. Applies to all full-time employees in Germany. Part-time and contractor entitlements are pro-rated; see your contract for specifics.

## Annual leave
Full-time employees are entitled to **30 working days** of paid annual leave per calendar year. Up to **5 unused days** may be carried over into the following year and must be taken by 31 March.

Annual leave is requested via the HR portal and approved by your direct manager.

## Sick leave
Employees are entitled to paid sick leave for up to **6 weeks** at full pay, per illness. Beyond that, statutory sickness benefits (Krankengeld) from the public health insurance fund apply.

A medical certificate ("Krankschreibung") is required **from day 3** onwards. For sick leave longer than 3 calendar days, submit the certificate to HR via the portal within 7 days of issuance.

## Parental leave
Parental leave follows the German Elternzeit framework: up to **3 years** of unpaid leave per parent, per child. Parental allowance (Elterngeld) is paid by the government for the first 14 months and is independent of Aurora's pay.

Notify HR at least 7 weeks before you intend to start parental leave.

## Sabbatical
After **5 years** of continuous employment, employees may apply for a sabbatical of up to **3 months**. Approval is at manager discretion and depends on team workload. Sabbaticals may be paid (deducted from accrued time) or unpaid.

## Bereavement leave
- Spouse, child, or parent: 5 working days paid leave.
- Sibling or grandparent: 2 working days paid leave.
- In-law: 1 working day paid leave.

## How to request leave
1. Open the HR portal at `hr.aurora.example`.
2. Navigate to **My Leave → Request leave**.
3. Pick the leave type, dates, and provide a short reason for sick or unpaid leave.
4. Your manager will approve or decline within 3 business days.

## Public holidays
Aurora Labs observes all German federal public holidays. The office closure between **24 December and 1 January** is paid and does not count against your annual leave allowance.
