# Aurora Labs — Code of Conduct

All Aurora Labs employees, contractors, and visitors are expected to follow this Code of Conduct at company premises and at any company-sponsored event.

## Respect
We treat each other with respect regardless of role, background, or seniority. Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation in any form is not tolerated.

## Reporting harassment
If you experience or witness harassment, you may report it through any of the following channels:

1. **Your manager** or skip-level manager.
2. **HR partner** — contact details are in the HR portal.
3. **Anonymous ethics hotline** — `ethics.aurora.example` or +49 30 1234 5678.
4. **Works council (Betriebsrat)** — for collective concerns.

All reports are investigated. Retaliation against people who report in good faith is itself a violation of this Code and will be treated as serious misconduct.

## Confidentiality
Employees handling personal data, customer information, or trade secrets must follow our Data Protection Policy and the GDPR. Unauthorized disclosure may result in termination and legal action.

Customer data must only be accessed for legitimate business reasons. All production database access is logged.

## Outside employment
Employees may take outside employment provided it does not conflict with Aurora Labs interests and does not interfere with their work performance. Outside employment must be disclosed to HR in writing.

## Conflicts of interest
Disclose any relationship (family, financial, romantic) with vendors, customers, or candidates that could create a conflict of interest. Disclosures are made to HR via the Conflicts portal.

## Substance policy
Alcohol is permitted at sanctioned company events in moderation. Illegal drugs are not permitted on company premises at any time. Employees who appear impaired at work will be sent home and may be subject to disciplinary action.

## Equipment and software
Company-issued equipment is for work-related use. Limited personal use is acceptable provided it does not interfere with work duties or expose the company to security or legal risk. Installing unlicensed software is prohibited.
