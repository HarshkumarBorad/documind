# Aurora Labs — Onboarding Handbook

Welcome to Aurora Labs. This handbook covers your first month with us.

## Day 1
- Pick up your laptop from the IT desk on Floor 3 between 09:00 and 10:00.
- HR will email your accounts list within 24 hours. Activate them via the company SSO portal at `sso.aurora.example`.
- Attend the 09:30 orientation in the Lakeshore Room (Floor 5).

## First-week checklist
1. Complete mandatory compliance training: GDPR, information security, anti-harassment. All courses are in the Learning Portal and take ~3 hours combined.
2. 1:1 with your manager — they'll book this in your first 48 hours.
3. 1:1 with your HR partner — booked by HR for end of week 1.
4. Set up your development environment using the Engineering Setup Guide in Confluence.
5. Join the relevant Slack channels for your team and tooling.

## Accounts you will receive
- Aurora SSO (email, calendar, drive).
- GitHub Enterprise organization access.
- Slack workspace.
- Aurora internal cloud console (`console.aurora.example`).
- VPN credentials (delivered separately by IT for security).

## Your buddy
Every new hire is paired with a "buddy" from a different team. Your buddy answers informal questions during your first month and is not in your reporting line. You can reach out to them anytime via Slack.

## Probation
The standard probation period is **six months**. During this time you will have monthly check-ins with your manager. At the end of probation, your manager conducts a formal review covering goals, performance, and team fit.

## Working hours
Aurora Labs operates a trust-based working-hours model. Core hours are 10:00–16:00; outside of that, organise your time as best suits your work and team. Remote work up to 3 days per week is supported for all eligible roles.

## Reporting and escalations
For onboarding issues, contact `onboarding@aurora.example` or your assigned HR partner. For urgent technical access issues, file a ticket via the Helpdesk portal at `helpdesk.aurora.example`.
